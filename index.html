Our Story

Thai Paragon Marrickville is Located on Victoria Road, Marrickville. Our funky and relaxing environment will make your dining experience extraordinary. Our modern Thai cuisine offers a wide range of specialties such a Duck Kaeng Phed Ped Yang, Lime Leaf and Peppercorn with King Prawn, Choo Chee Prawn and many more.

We are open 7days a week. Our lunch special serving between 11.00am to 3.00pm. You can dine in or order pick up or have it home delivered for free between 05.00pm, and 9.30pm. We look forward to serving you. Now Thai Paragon Marrickville is a fully licensed restaurant (B.Y.O. wine only)
